Botanicat

"Botanicat" is a game created for the second-year of the Cnam ENJMIN Computer Science bachelor's degree.
Creation info:
  • Team: 4 people
  • Time: 8 months
  • Creation context: Cnam ENJMIN computer science license second year project.
Pitch:

In this game, you play an expert in floracat and a weather fairy.
The floracats, adorable half-flower half-cat creatures, need a perfect environment to florist.
Your goal is to develop your greenhouse so your floracats find their balance thanks to the weather ans their friends.
The happier they get, the more points you win. Try to win as much as you can.
Game Description:
  • Genre: Card game - Strategy
  • Multiplayer : Co-op - two players
  • Languages : French, English, Swedish
